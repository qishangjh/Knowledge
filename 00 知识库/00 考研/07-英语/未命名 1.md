**适用场景：** 会议记录。 (较少考，但需掌握格式)

**格式结构：**

> **Time:** [Date and Time] **Place:** [Location] **Present:** [Attendees] **Presided by:** [Host's Name]
>
> **Body:** The meeting focused on [Topic]. **Firstly,** [Person A] proposed/stated that… **Secondly,** [Person B] argued/suggested that… **Lastly,** a conclusion was reached that…
>
> **Submitted by:** Li Ming **Date:** [Date]

# 第二部分：短文/大作文（Part B 英语一图画作文）

考研英语一大作文标准结构为 **三段式**。

## 第一段：图画描述段 (Description)

**功能：** 客观描述画面 + 引用文字说明。

**万能句型：**

1. **开篇：**
    - **As is symbolically illustrated in the portrayal, [describe the scene].**
    - **Graphically depicted in the cartoon is/are [Person/Item + Action].**
    - **What catches my attention most in the illustration is [Focus].**
2. **细节/对比 (左右图)：**
    - **In the left picture, [Action A], while in the right one, [Action B].**
    - **…consequently giving rise to a sharp contrast.** (形成鲜明对比)
3. **文字说明 (Caption)：**
    - **The caption under the cartoon reads: "…"**

## 第二段：意图阐释/论证段 (Interpretation)

**功能：** 揭示寓意 + 分析原因/后果 (核心段落)。

**万能句型：**

1. **主题句 (Topic Sentence)：**
    - **Simple as the drawing seems, it conveys a thought-provoking message: [Thesis Statement, e.g., persistence is the key to success].**
    - **The primary aim of this picture is to illustrate that [Main Idea].**
2. **原因分析 (Universal Reasons - 论证三板斧)：**
    - _过渡词：_ **The fundamental factors contributing to this phenomenon can be explained as follows.**
    - **原因一 (经济/科技)：** **To begin with, with the rapid development of economy/technology/society, [Explain how tech/money affects the topic].** (例如：人们生活水平提高，或科技让生活更便利但也带来惰性)
    - **原因二 (意识/心态)：** **Moreover, people are becoming increasingly aware of the importance of [Topic].** (正面话题) / **Some people lack the consciousness of [Topic].** (负面话题)
    - **原因三 (教育/家庭)：** **Finally, parental overprotection / lack of proper education serves as a contributing factor.** (常用于青年人话题)

## 第三段：评论/建议段 (Comments & Conclusion)

**功能：** 总结观点 + 提出建议/展望未来。

**万能句型：**

1. **总结：**
    - **In conclusion/In light of the above, it is evident that [Restate Topic] is of great importance.**
2. **建议/措施 (Measures)：**
    - **Accordingly, it is high time that measures were taken.**
    - **On the macro level, the government/media should issue strict laws / launch extensive campaigns to [Action].**
    - **On the micro level, individuals should enhance their awareness of [Topic].**
3. **展望 (Outlook)：**
    - **Only by doing so can we build a harmonious society / embrace a brighter future.**

# 第三部分：高分加分亮点词句 (Universal Material)

从资料中提取的**万能主题词**和**原因**，可直接填入模版：

- **个人品质类 (Personal Qualities):**
    - Persistence (坚持)
    - Self-confidence (自信)
    - Spirit of craftsmanship (工匠精神)
    - Critical thinking (批判性思维)
- **社会热点类 (Social Issues):**
    - Environmental protection / Low-carbon life (环保/低碳)
    - Cultural heritage protection (文化遗产保护)
    - Information explosion (信息爆炸)
    - Artificial Intelligence (人工智能)
- **常用论证句 (可用于第二段):**
    - **Technology is a double-edged sword.** (科技是把双刃剑)
    - **It is undeniable that modern individuals face challenges of ever-increasing complexity.** (现代人面临日益复杂的挑战)
    - **This phenomenon is naturally associated with young people.** (这种现象与年轻人自然相关)

**使用建议：**

1. **背诵框架：** 先熟练掌握"首段描述+中段三点论证+尾段建议"的骨架。
2. **填充内容：** 遇到不同话题（如环保、坚持、合作），替换第二段中的关键词（红字部分）和具体原因。
3. **灵活调整：** 小作文注意语域（给教授写信要正式，给朋友写信可亲切）；大作文注意时态（描述图画用现在时/进行时）。
